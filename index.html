    Some people are hard to hear

    ...through no fault of their own. Many people need the assistance of technology in order for their voices to be heard. Unfortunately, a lot of that technology is hard to use, is hard to configure, and is crazy expensive. We want to fix that.

    Why "CoughDrop"? Cough drops help you get back the voice you already had, but that maybe people couldn't hear so well. They're cheap and nobody looks at you weird when you use them. We want to help get AAC to that same point by providing affordable, approachable, simple solutions to complex communication needs.

    We're still in the very beginnings, here, but stay with us and you're in for a treat. Follow us on Twitter for more information
